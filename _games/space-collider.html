Pilot a spaceship and protect the Earth from waves of alien invaders and incoming asteroids!

Features

  • • Campaign with 13 levels
  • • Aliens move and shoot faster with each wave in Survival Mode
  • • Randomized alien group spawns in Survival Mode
  • • 4 Survival Mode difficulties (Easy, Normal, Hard and NIGHTMARE!)
  • • New alien groups
  • • Asteroids which can split into smaller asteroids when destroyed
  • • 5 new powerups
