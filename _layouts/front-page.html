About Us

Where Innovation Meets Play.

Sirius Game Studio is an independent gaming company based in Bangladesh. As an one-person team, we strive to create fun and engaging games for the players.


Founded in 2015 by Wasik Farhan Roopkotha and Cynthia Farheen Risha, our mission is to provide high-quality games that are accessible to everyone. We believe that games should be a source of joy and relaxation, and we aim to deliver that experience through our games. Our team is passionate about creating games that are not only entertaining but also challenging and rewarding.


This company was originally called "Roopkotha Studio" but was later rebranded to "Sirius Game Studio" in 2022. The name is inspired by the brightest star in the sky, Sirius, which symbolizes our commitment to excellence and innovation. Note that we are not an official company.
